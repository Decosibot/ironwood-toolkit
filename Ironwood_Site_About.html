About Ironwood

Built on Accountability.
Driven by Results.

Ironwood is an Atlanta-based real estate and renovation company built on a simple belief: do the work right, be transparent about the cost, and show up every day. That's it.

Our Story

How Ironwood Started

Ironwood was founded in Atlanta with a clear purpose: to fix what's broken about how renovation and real estate services are delivered to regular people. Too many homeowners have been burned by contractors who disappeared mid-project, or sellers who felt pressured into bad deals with no transparency.

We built Ironwood around two lines of business - buying homes from motivated sellers who need speed and simplicity, and delivering renovation work to homeowners who deserve a contractor they can actually trust. Both lines share the same core commitment: written scopes, transparent pricing, and no surprises.

We operate in Atlanta and the surrounding metro because this is our home. We know the neighborhoods, we understand the market, and we're invested in the outcome of every job we take. When a project has our name on it, we stand behind it - from the first phone call to the punch list.

We're not the biggest contractor in Atlanta. We're focused on being the most reliable one in every community we serve.
