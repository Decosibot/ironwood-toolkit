Investment Strategy
Fix & Flip
Acquire distressed residential properties, execute targeted renovations, and resell at full-market ARV.
Target: All-in ≤ 70% ARV  |  90–180 day cycle
BRRRR
Buy → Rehab → Rent → Refinance → Repeat. DSCR financing used to recycle capital into the next acquisition.
Target DSCR: ≥ 1.25  |  Long-term hold
Contract Remodel
Revenue-generating renovation services for homeowners via Landon Renovations while the portfolio builds scale.
Active revenue stream  |  Funds deal pipeline
