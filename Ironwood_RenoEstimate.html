⚙️ GHL Webhook Integration Guide — Renovation Pre-Estimate Page

1
Create GHL Webhook Trigger
GHL → Automation → New Workflow → Trigger: Inbound Webhook. Copy the webhook URL provided.
2
Paste Webhook URL
Replace YOUR_GHL_RENO_WEBHOOK_URL in the JS. Payload includes: firstName, lastName, phone, email, address, projectDescription (raw text the client typed), projectType + projectLabel (auto-detected), scope (array of detected items), scopeLabels (readable names), spaceSize, condition, finishLevel, budget, timeline, notes, estimateLow, estimateHigh, estimateRange, source, refNum, submittedAt, all UTM params.
3
Map GHL Fields + Custom Fields
Standard: firstName, lastName, phone, email, address. Custom fields: projectType, estimateRange (estimateLow – estimateHigh), scope, condition, finishLevel, budget, timeline.
4
Pipeline Action
Add to Pipeline → Pipeline: Reno Leads → Stage: New Lead. Tag: website-reno-lead. If utm_source = facebook_ad add tag fb-ad-lead for attribution tracking.
5
Automation Sequence
SMS: "Hi [firstName], your Ironwood estimate for your [projectType] is $[estimateLow]–$[estimateHigh]. We'll be in touch within 24 hours to schedule your free walkthrough. Ref: [refNum]". Email: full estimate summary. 24hr follow-up if no response.
6
Ad UTM Tracking
Append to ad destination URL: ?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=reno-leads-atlanta. JS captures and includes in payload automatically for full ad-to-lead attribution in GHL.
7
Lead Scoring Tip
Use GHL workflow conditions: if budget = over100 → assign to Ty immediately (high-value lead). If timeline = as_soon_as_possible → send SMS within 2 minutes not 5. Prioritize hot leads automatically.
