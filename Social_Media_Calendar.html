Property Profile

1960s–1990s stock, 3/2 or 4/2, 1,200–2,200 sqft. ARV $200K–$450K. Suburban/exurban. Needs work — deferred maintenance, dated finishes, or structural issues.

Seller Avatar

Inherited property, tired landlord, divorce/job loss, elderly downsizing, pre-foreclosure. All value speed and certainty over top dollar. Often overwhelmed and decision-fatigued.

Buyer / Investor Avatar

Cash buyer or HML-backed flipper. Owns 2–10 rentals. Wants a trusted contractor who shows up and doesn't surprise them on price. Values reliability over lowest bid.
