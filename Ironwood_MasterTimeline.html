Deals & Revenue
Residential Flips
Do Now
Buy distressed, renovate, sell. Core revenue engine. Every flip funds the next deal and builds the track record needed for rentals and investors.
WhenLaunch — ongoing
NeedsHML + Jeff crew + deal pipeline
Upside$25K–$60K net per deal
HonestYou already have everything to start
Remodel Contracts
Do Now
Renovation work for homeowners. Immediate cash flow while you build the acquisition pipeline. Keeps Jeff and crew working between flips.
WhenDay 1
NeedsClient Service Agreement + Scope of Work + marketing
Upside$8K–$30K net per job
HonestSimplest revenue line — start immediately
Wholesale / Assignment
Moderate
Find a deal, put it under contract, assign it to another investor for a fee. No rehab needed. Good for leads that don't fit Ironwood's buy box.
WhenMonths 6–12
NeedsBuyer list + Ty's license (helps) + contract knowledge
Upside$3K–$15K per assignment, zero rehab risk
HonestEasy money on leads you'd pass on anyway
New Construction
Complex
Ground-up residential builds. High margin but requires GC license, land entitlement, longer timelines, and significantly more capital.
WhenYear 4+ / Jeff GC license active
NeedsJeff GC license + land + construction financing + permits
Upside$50K–$150K+ per build
HonestDon't rush this. Rehab expertise first.
Long-Term Rentals
BRRRR #1 — First Rental
Moderate
Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat. The wealth-building engine. Cash-out refi funds the next deal. First one takes the most work — after that the system runs.
WhenMonths 6–12 (DSCR lender locked first)
NeedsDSCR lender + 70% ARV deal + stable tenant
Upside$400–$800/mo cash flow + equity + refi capital
HonestStart lender conversations now, before you have the deal
Section 8 / VASH Tenants
Do Now
Accept Housing Choice Vouchers (Section 8) or VA Supportive Housing (VASH) tenants on your rentals. Government pays rent directly to you — most reliable rent stream available.
WhenFirst rental — no additional work
NeedsProperty passes HUD inspection (minor) + landlord registration
UpsideGuaranteed govt payment + mission impact + low vacancy
HonestEasiest giving-back move. Zero extra complexity.

Vacation Rentals
VR #1 — Blue Ridge / Ellijay / Dahlonega GA
Moderate
North GA mountain cabin STR. Easier market to enter than FL coast — lower purchase price, less HOA restriction, strong year-round occupancy driven by Atlanta proximity.
WhenYear 2 / after BRRRR #1 cash-flowing
NeedsSTR permit (county-level) + STR insurance + DSCR or HML
Upside$225–$350/night · 65–70% occ · $4,400–$7,000/mo gross
HonestBest first VR market. Start here before FL coast.
VR — Destin / 30A / PCB FL
Moderate
Florida Emerald Coast beach STR. Higher nightly rates and occupancy than GA mountains but more competition, stricter STR regulations, and higher purchase prices.
WhenYear 3 / after GA VR proven
NeedsFL STR license + HOA clearance + FL insurance (higher)
Upside$300–$600/night · 70–75% occ · $6,300–$13,500/mo gross
HonestHigher ceiling, higher cost. Do GA first.

Moderate

WhenMonth 3–6 / before BRRRR #1

HonestBuild the relationship early — it takes time to get approved

Moderate
Each rental property (especially VR) in its own single-purpose LLC for liability isolation. Ironwood LLC holds the membership interests in each. ~$500/ea in Delaware.
WhenAs each rental/VR property is acquired
NeedsRegistered agent + $500 formation + Unanimous Consent
HonestDo this from the start — don't move properties in later

Outside Passive Investors

HonestDon't shortcut the legal. PPM protects you.
REIT Evaluation
Long Game
Convert to or form a Real Estate Investment Trust. Requires 90% income distribution, 100+ shareholders, and significant compliance overhead. Only makes sense at major scale.
When20+ doors / $5M+ portfolio
NeedsIndependent legal + tax counsel + both members agree
HonestDon't think about this before Phase 4. Not before.

Giving Back — Social Impact Initiatives
"Ironwood LLC believes that real estate wealth should create opportunity, not just accumulate it. Through affordable housing, veteran support, reentry employment, and community investment, we build more than properties — we build stability for people who need it most."
🏠 Section 8 / VASH Housing
Accept Housing Choice Vouchers and VA Supportive Housing vouchers on Ironwood rentals. Government pays rent directly — most reliable payment stream available. Zero additional complexity. Helps veterans and low-income families simultaneously.
🟢 Do on first rental — no extra work required
🔨 Hire First — Reentry Workers
Jeff's crew hires parolees through Georgia Justice Project and Honest Jobs. Government subsidizes wages during training period. Workers get a trade skill and employment record. Ironwood gets reliable labor with reduced cost. Win on every side.
🟢 Call Georgia Justice Project — start now
🏘️ Transitional Housing Partnership
Partner with a Georgia reentry or veteran nonprofit. They screen and manage tenants, provide wrap-around services, and often have grant-backed rent payments. You own the asset and provide the housing. No property management burden on Ironwood.
🟡 Year 2 — reach out to Atlanta Mission or similar
🎖️ Dedicated Veteran Unit
Designate one unit or property specifically for VASH voucher veterans. Partner with the local VA office. Creates a consistent relationship, fills vacancies faster, and builds a story that strengthens the Ironwood brand in the community.
🟡 Year 2–3 alongside rental portfolio build
💰 LIHTC — Low Income Housing Tax Credit
Federal and state tax credits for qualifying affordable housing projects. Complex 10-year compliance period but generates significant tax offset. Worth evaluating when the portfolio reaches 10+ units and Holdings is activated.
🔴 Year 4–5 — requires tax counsel + CPA
🌱 Ironwood Foundation 501(c)(3)
Nonprofit arm that takes donations, applies for grants, and partners with the LLC to renovate and provide transitional housing. The LLC does the rehab work (paid at market rate), the Foundation holds the mission. This is how large operators formalize their community work.
⚫ Year 8+ — build the story informally first
