4Terms & Conditions
  1. This agreement is contingent upon the referred client closing a real estate transaction with the Receiving Agent within 12 months of the referral date.
  2. The Receiving Agent agrees to keep the Referring Party reasonably informed of the transaction status upon request, including offer acceptance, contract execution, and anticipated closing date.
  3. The Referring Agent (Ty Jackson) is acting solely in a referral capacity and will not provide any real estate brokerage services to the referred client in connection with this transaction.
  4. This agreement is entered into in compliance with Georgia Real Estate Commission rules and regulations governing the payment of referral fees between licensed real estate professionals.
  5. If the referred client engages in multiple transactions with the Receiving Agent within the 12-month period, the referral fee applies to the first closing only, unless the parties agree otherwise in a separate written addendum.
  6. Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice to the other if no real estate transaction has been initiated within 6 months of the referral date.
  7. Any dispute arising under this agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration conducted in the State of Georgia. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney's fees and costs.
  8. This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Georgia and the rules and regulations of the Georgia Real Estate Commission.
5Georgia License Compliance Notice

Both parties to this agreement represent and warrant that they hold active, unrestricted Georgia real estate licenses (or are otherwise exempt from licensure requirements pursuant to applicable law). Referral fees may only be paid to and between licensed real estate professionals. This agreement does not authorize either party to provide real estate brokerage services on behalf of the other party, nor does it create a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship between the parties.
