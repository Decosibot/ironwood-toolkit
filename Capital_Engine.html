How to Use Each Play — Phase Guide
Phase 1 — Now
Wholesale + Labor
Lock deals below market, assign the contract. Anchor Pro quotes full market rate — Ironwood executes at crew cost. Both spreads require zero capital.
Phase 2 — Month 3–6
HML + Private Money
Use HML for flips. Raise private money from investors at 8–10%, deploy on deals returning 20%+. Each deal you close builds the track record for the next raise.
Phase 3 — Year 1+
Creative Finance + BRRRR
Sub-to and seller finance plays let you acquire below-market debt. BRRRR recycles the same capital forever. Rate and equity spreads compound over time.
