Site Overview

Strategy, audience paths, site map, and brand rules for the full website.

Primary Goal: Generate two types of leads — motivated sellers who want to sell as-is, and homeowners who want renovation work done. Both paths end at a GHL form and enter separate pipelines. The site presents Ironwood Renovations as one brand — no partner names, no internal structure, just the company.

Brand Rules for the Website

Do Use

  • Ironwood Renovations (primary brand name)
  • Ironwood LLC (legal footer only)
  • Georgia · Licensed · Insured
  • Black / cream / gold color palette
  • Strong, direct copy — no fluff
  • Real before/after photos when available

Do Not Use

  • Ty Jackson, Jeff Sarabia, Jordan Sarabia (no partner names)
  • Anchor Pro Solutions / Landon Renovations
  • Delaware LLC / foreign qualification language
  • Any waterfall, profit split, or internal structure
  • Ironwood Holdings (investor brand — separate)
  • Generic stock photos of random contractors

The Ironwood Offer Process
Section▼
Removes fear of the unknown. Sellers hesitate because they don't know what happens after they call. Walk them through it step by step.

3-Step Process

  • Step 1 — Tell Us About Your Property: Fill out the short form below. Takes 2 minutes. No commitment required.
  • Step 2 — We Schedule a Visit: We come see the property — usually within a few days. Quick, no-pressure walkthrough.
  • Step 3 — Receive a Fair Cash Offer: We present a written offer within 24–48 hours. You decide — no obligation, ever.

Seller Lead Form → GHL
Form → GHL▼
The primary conversion point on this page. Embedded GHL form. Short — only ask what's needed to follow up. Longer intake happens on the phone.

Form Fields

  • First Name (required)
  • Last Name (required)
  • Phone (required — GHL uses for SMS)
  • Email (required)
  • Property Address (required)
  • Situation / How Can We Help? (dropdown)
  • How Soon Are You Looking to Sell? (dropdown)

GHL Integration

  • Pipeline: "Seller Leads" → Stage: New Lead
  • Trigger: Automated SMS within 5 min of submission
  • SMS: "Hi [Name], this is Ironwood Renovations. Thanks for reaching out — we'll call you within 24 hours to discuss your property at [Address]."
  • Email: Confirmation + what to expect next
  • Tag in GHL: "website-seller-lead"
Embed GHL form via iframe or native Webflow integration. Form submissions go directly into GHL CRM pipeline.

FAQ — Seller
Section▼
Addresses the top objections before they become reasons not to call. Accordion-style expandable questions.

FAQ Questions

  • Will you lowball me? — "Our offer is based on the home's current condition and local market. We're transparent about how we arrive at our number."
  • Do I need to make any repairs? — "No. We buy as-is. Leave everything exactly as it is."
  • Do I need a real estate agent? — "No agent needed — and no commissions. We work directly with you."
  • How fast can you actually close? — "As fast as 14 days once we're under contract. Or we work around your timeline."
  • Is there any obligation when I fill out the form? — "Zero. You're just starting a conversation."
  • What areas do you buy in? — "We buy throughout Georgia. Submit your address and we'll confirm."

GHL Setup Checklist for the Website

Pipelines to Create in GHL

  • Seller Leads — stages: New Lead / Contacted / Appointment Set / Offer Made / Under Contract / Closed / Dead
  • Reno Leads — stages: New Lead / Contacted / Walkthrough Scheduled / Quote Sent / CSA Signed / In Progress / Complete
  • General Inquiries — stages: New / Responded / Converted / Closed

Automations to Build in GHL

  • Seller lead submitted → SMS within 5 min + email confirmation
  • Reno lead submitted → SMS within 5 min + email confirmation
  • No response after 24hr → follow-up SMS
  • No response after 72hr → second follow-up
  • Appointment confirmed → reminder SMS day before + 1hr before
  • Deal closed → request Google review via GHL
