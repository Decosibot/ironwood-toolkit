The Estimate Process

How the Estimate Works

No forms with 40 questions. Just describe what you want in plain English - our system handles the rest.

Step 01
Describe It in Plain English
Tell us what you want done in your own words. "I want to gut my kitchen and put in new cabinets, quartz countertops, and LVP flooring" is all we need to get started. No technical jargon required.
Step 02
System Detects the Scope
Our estimator reads your description and identifies the project type, materials, and scope automatically - then generates an instant ballpark range based on current Atlanta-area pricing.
Step 03
See Your Range Instantly
You get an honest low-to-high range on the spot - before anyone visits your home or calls you. Then we schedule a free walkthrough to build the written scope and firm price.
