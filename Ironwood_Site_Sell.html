Who We Help

Every Situation Is Different - We Understand That

Whether your situation is complicated or straightforward, Ironwood works with motivated sellers in all circumstances.

Inherited Property
Inherited a home you don't want to manage or maintain? We handle probate-friendly purchases and make the process simple during a difficult time.
Behind on Payments
Facing foreclosure or falling behind on your mortgage? A fast cash sale can stop the clock and protect your credit before things escalate.
Divorce / Life Change
When life changes quickly, a shared property can complicate things. We buy fast so both parties can move on cleanly and without delay.
Relocating Quickly
Job transfer or out-of-state move coming up fast? Don't deal with showings and months on the market - we close on your schedule.
Property Needs Major Repairs
Foundation issues, roof damage, fire or water damage - we buy homes exactly as they are. You don't spend a dollar before closing.
Tired Landlord
Done dealing with tenants, maintenance calls, and vacancies? We purchase rental properties with or without tenants in place.
The Process

Simple from Start to Close

Four steps is all it takes. No guesswork, no delays, no surprises.

01
Submit Your Info Online
